Certifications

AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate
The AWS Solutions Architect certification demonstrates the ability to design and deploy scalable, fault-tolerant systems on AWS. This certification includes in-depth knowledge of cloud architecture and AWS services such as EC2, S3, RDS, VPC, and IAM.
Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)
The CCNA certification validates the ability to install, configure, and troubleshoot networks using Cisco devices. The certification covers topics such as routing and switching, IP addressing, network security, and troubleshooting methodologies.
